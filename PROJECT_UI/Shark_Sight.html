Welcome to SharkSight, an ocean risk assessment system prototype developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Disclaimer
This is a demonstration prototype. All data displayed including chlorophyll concentrations, sea surface temperatures, shark presence indicators, and risk assessments are simulated dummy data for demonstration purposes only. Do not use this information for actual marine navigation or safety decisions.
